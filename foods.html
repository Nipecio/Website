My Favorite Foods!

1. Veggie Spring Rolls

Veggies spring rolls are one of my most favorite foods because they outer shell tastes amazing and so good. Everytime I see one I always take it. Another thing is, is that it's very healthy for you considering it has a load of vegeetables and they make it most livel if you know which vegetables to put in!

2. Momo's

Momo's couldn't be a better food since they are easy to consume and bit size. Momo's are very similar to veggie spring rolls and have a nice and soft outer shell and veggies in the insde. The one main key differnce is that you can style it in any way you want. They look like they came out of a fantasy story!

3. Manchurian Soup

This unique liquid soup is my favorite Chinese meal ever. it has a small amount of veggetables and it's broth tastes so good that it feels like it came from somewhere else. It's super easy to make and sometime you can add these hard noodles that soften up as you leave them in there. SLURP!
